IEEE PIMRC 2026 Workshop

Workshop on Integrated Sensing and Communication for Low-Altitude UAVs

Singapore, 1 September 2026

About the Workshop

Next generation wireless networks are envisioned to seamlessly integrate communications, localization, ranging, imaging, and environment awareness within a unified platform. In particular, low-altitude Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) equipped with Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) offer unprecedented spatial agility and high-resolution Earth observation capabilities.

This workshop focuses on the critical scientific issues and technical roadmaps for enabling Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC) in UAV platforms. We aim to bring together researchers to discuss waveform design, SAR imaging algorithms, resource allocation, and the integration of these technologies in practical 5G/6G aerial systems.

Topics of Interest

  • Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imaging algorithms for low-altitude UAVs
  • Joint waveform design for communication and sensing using OFDM signals
  • Range Cell Migration (RCM) correction and beam pattern optimization
  • Performance metrics evaluation (e.g., NMSE, ISLR, and PEL) in ISAC systems
  • Resource allocation and interference management for multi-functional aerial networks
  • Architectures and transmission protocols for UAV-to-ground and UAV-to-UAV ISAC
  • Edge intelligence and learning for joint power, rate, and beam control
  • Systems, simulation codes, testbeds, and over-the-air validation for 5G/6G integration
